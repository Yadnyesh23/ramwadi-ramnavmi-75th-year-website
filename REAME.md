# Ramwadi Ramnavmi 75th Year Website

This website is the **official online presence of Ramwadi Mandal**, created to celebrate and showcase the **75th year of Ram Navmi celebrations**.

The platform provides information about the festival, events, history, and activities organized by the Mandal. It acts as a digital space where devotees and visitors can stay updated and connected with Ramwadi Mandal.

---

## Features

- Information about Ramwadi Mandal
- Details about Ram Navmi celebrations
- Event updates and announcements
- Photo gallery of celebrations
- Contact and location information
- Mobile-friendly and responsive design

---

## Purpose of the Website

The goal of this website is to:
- Digitally represent Ramwadi Mandal
- Share festival updates with the community
- Preserve the history of Ram Navmi celebrations
- Make it easier for people to access information online
